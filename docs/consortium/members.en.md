# Consortium Members

## Overview

The DEMOS consortium consists of researchers from multiple German institutions and one international associated partner, covering all major physics domains within ErUM (Exploration of the Universe and Matter).

## Key Leadership Roles

Consortium members hold leadership positions in major experimental collaborations:

- **ATLAS** and **CMS** (TUDO, LMU, TUM)
- **LHCb** and **COMPASS** (RUB-H)
- **BESIII** (JGU)
- **Belle II** (MPP, LMU)
- **IceCube**, **KamLAND**, **MINOS**, **Daya Bay** (TUM, RUB-A)
- **GERDA**, **MAJORANA**, **LEGEND** (MPP)

## Software Development Leadership

Consortium members are leading developers of widely-used tools:

- **PyHF** (TUM, LMU, SC)
- **BAT.jl** (multiple institutions)
- **ROOT/RooFit** ecosystem contributors (TUDO)
- **HS3** standard developers (TUDO, TUM, LMU, SC)
- **Amplitude serialization** pioneers (RUB-H)
